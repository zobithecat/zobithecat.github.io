Selected open-source projects

airspy-mini-dmb 2026

End-to-end open-source software receiver for Korean Terrestrial DMB on low-cost SDR hardware.

Pure-Python pipeline: ETI parsing → Fast Information Channel decode → Reed-Solomon (204, 188) + Forney convolutional deinterleaver outer FEC → MPEG-4 SL packet demux → H.264 elementary stream reconstruction → ffmpeg decode. Verified on a 30 MB on-air capture of YTN DMB (channel K8B, 183.008 MHz) with an Airspy Mini and an indoor antenna: 87.3 % of Reed-Solomon blocks decode cleanly and ffmpeg renders QVGA H.264 frames containing recognisable Korean broadcast content.

Papers (preprint + Korean companion) are in the paper/ directory.

Stack: Python · C++ · LaTeX · Airspy Mini SDR · ETI(NI) · ETSI EN 300 401 · ETSI TS 102 427/428 · TTAK.KO-07.0026 · ISO/IEC 14496-1

Research areas (from patents)

Broadcasting & streaming media

Joint-broadcasting devices, gesture-controlled personal broadcasting, metadata-driven streaming video editing, and (current side work) software-defined T-DMB reception end-to-end.

Sports performance analytics

Multi-year programme of training and start-phase analysis for the Korean national bobsled / luge teams: technique- training systems, multi-camera time sync via optical time codes, start-phase analytic data generation, performance analysis pipelines, and steerable immersive content for motion simulators.

Wearable health sensing

Machine-learning-based SpO2 measurement on wearable devices, and biosignal extraction from users in motion — pushing PPG-style sensing past the usual stationary assumption.

Computer vision & privacy

Pose-aware video portrait-right protection (faces/identifying-pose redaction), and panoramic indoor scene synthesis.

IoT & safety systems

Worksite-hazard warning device, device authentication for IoT networks, and pattern-display window systems.
